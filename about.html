About Us

There are times in life when our experience of trauma, loss and change begins to impact the way we see the world, the way we interact, and even the way we see ourselves.

Many clients describe feeling ‘stuck’ and unable to move forward. Evoke the Mind Counselling can help you make sense of your thoughts, feelings and body sensations.

A healthy emotional and mental state helps us manage stressful situations, and can also allow for personal growth. We draw on a range of therapies which are evidenced and strength based.
