What can I expect at the first session with Evoke the Mind Counselling?

  • To meet with a qualified counselling practitioner who listens with care and without judgement, meeting you at your pace.
  • To feel met and supported as you are. Our approach is neuro‑affirming and honours every expression of identity.
  • To feel emotionally safe as we explore what’s on your mind and begin to understand the concerns you’re carrying.
  • To receive support in processing your experience, with compassion and clarity.
  • To gently work towards new ways of thinking that align with your needs, strengths and sense of self.
