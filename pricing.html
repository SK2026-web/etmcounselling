Our Pricing

Face to face, video and phone consultations are available with Evoke the Mind Counselling.

If you are an NDIS participant and would like to access counselling therapy through your plan, please call to discuss. Alternatively, you can contact your NDIS plan manager to see if you're eligible.

Referrals are always welcome and you’re welcome to get in touch in whatever way feels most comfortable for you. While Medicare rebates aren’t available for our counselling services, we’re here to offer steady, compassionate support whenever you need it.
